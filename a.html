  We're teaching about how to make Website with HTML.
HTML(Hyper Text Markup Language)is the most commonly used language for creating sites.

  First, students simply drew the site they wanted to create on paper to design the site. Then we taught students how to use tags used in HTML and how to decorate them using Style. Finally, they applied this to create their own site.
We are very grateful for the students' passionate participation in the class.
